What we do

We work to empower developers to build high quality, interactive, and beautiful mobile apps with the web technologies they already know and love.

We believe there’s a huge opportunity to bring the web to mobile and that we are the company to do it best.

What we look for

We’re always searching for smart, driven, and talented people to join our team in Madison, San Francisco, or remotely (for specified positions only).

We look for great communicators who have the ability to empathize with others, and a passion for learning.

Why Ionic?

We’re not your typical tech company. We weren’t built in the valley - or by it. We’re a developer built, design focused, product obsessed team of open sourcers.

A healthy city to call home

Our HQ is located in downtown Madison, WI. The city’s bike-friendliness, affordability, and world-class University make it a great place to set down roots.

We <3 open source

Our team is dedicated to producing world-class open source projects. We engage with our community and welcome feedback and pull requests.

Have ownership

At Ionic, we know our teammates are best in class at what they do. That’s why they're given trust, independence, and ownership over their work.

A welcoming environment

Ionic is a welcoming place for everyone, and we do our best to make sure our teammates feel supported and connected here at all times.

Make an impact

Work on open source projects and products that are used by millions of people all over the world. Help change the way the world builds mobile.

Love what you do

More than anything else, Ionic is a company that wants you to love what you do. We take pride in creating a place where people love to work.
